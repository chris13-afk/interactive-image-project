Nona's Mission

See Our Pizza Our family-owned restaurant showcases multiple styles of pizza from around the world. We feature only the best ingredients, highest quality cheeses, and fresh dough made by hand daily. See our pizzas!

El Diablo

Fresh mozzarella, peppadew peppers, soppressata, dollops of ricotta, crushed red pepper. Finished with “Fire in the Hive” local hot honey drizzle and basil after cooking.

The Garden Pesto

Fresh spinach, roasted cherry tomatoes, artichokes and black olives with a checkerboard of house-made pesto and finished with fresh basil.

The Bee's Knees

No sauce, red onion, finished with shaved parmesan, prosciutto, arugula, and “Fire in the Hive” local hot honey drizzle.

Death by Pepperoni

Pepperoni, Rosa Grande pepperoni, and Cup’n’Char pepperoni with smoked provolone, topped with two stripes of marinara.

The Pan-Demic

No sauce, hot soppressata, cup n char pepperoni, fresh jalapeño, hot cherry peppers and piped ricotta finished with local “Fire in the Hive” hot honey drizzle

Regular Toppings

Pepperoni, Italian Sausage, Bacon, Ham, Mushrooms, Cherry tomatoes, Red Onion, Black Olives, Fresh Jalapeno, Pineapple, Whole Roasted Garlic, Meatballs, Rosa Grande Pepperoni, Spinach, Banana Peppers, Pesto

Margherita

San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, olive oil and fresh basil. Winner of the 2011 “Best pizza in America” at the American Pizza Championship.

Quattro Formaggi

No sauce, fresh mozzarella, gorgonzola, ricotta and parmesan

The Marinara

No cheese, San Marzano tomatoes, whole-roasted garlic, oregano & EVOO. Finished with basil after cooking.
